Riot API Challenge 2.0 - Project Documentation Part 3

Posted on 8/24/2015

Yesterday, Sunday the 23rd of August, I went to the NALCS Summer Split finals viewing that was hosted by ESN in Toronto. You can see more about the event here. Since it was endorsed by Riot, you can imagine that there was a lot of cool stuff being given out - I managed to get myself a poro! Anyways, before I get too much into the event I'd like to give a bit of a preface on me and CLG.

Well over three years ago I was avidly playing League of Legends. In June of 2012 I decided to tune into the MLG Raleigh competition - that was where I first saw CLG. While they didn't win, after watching them that day I decided CLG was the right team for me. That was a pretty good year for CLG as they made the season 2 world finals, my first worlds event. There have been a lot of different faces on the roster since then and a lot of hardship for the team, but one constant was my favourite player Doublelift, so you can imagine my excitement when I got myself a Doublelift Jersey to support my favourite player and team.



Last night at the NALCS finals viewing, I was more excited for CLG than I had ever been - and they delivered. I watched CLG go to worlds over 3 years ago, and I got to do it again last night. To give you an idea of just how important this was to me, I recorded a video at the tail end of game 3 at the viewing party last night - you can hear myself and my friends (along with the whole crowd) go beserk when CLG closed out the series.


Headphone user warning! Our passion for CLG is pretty loud!


As you can imagine, between the EULCS finals and NALCS finals yesterday, no programming was done. I'll be getting back to work on the Riot API Challenge and finishing up my website in the coming days. I admit yesterday while watching the EULCS finals game 5 at the beginning of the viewing party (bonus!), I saw xPeke build Nashor's Tooth on Azir and it warmed my heart after studying the changes on it myself for this project. You can expect to find out about that and more on my completed website!

The API Challenge ends in just over a week today so you can expect my final post sometime around then.


CLG IS GOING TO WORLDS BABY!
